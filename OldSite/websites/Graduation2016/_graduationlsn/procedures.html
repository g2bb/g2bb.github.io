Graduation Processional/Ceremony Procedures

  • Graduates will line up in the Assembly Room with their mentors. Again, the only things you should have with you in the room are those items that can be with you during the ceremony in the auditorium.
  • Each senior will be issued a card with his/her name typed on it to be read at graduation.
  • Seniors will line up in pairs at the north ramp to enter for the processional.
  • Guests will not be allowed admittance to the auditorium once the graduates gather in the North Foyer for the processional. Once the processional is over, guests with tickets may enter or re-enter.
  • Mentors and graduates will need to follow the lead of the ushers to enter the auditorium. Graduates will enter in pairs down the center aisle and fill in from the center aisle to the outside. Seating will be reserved.
  • Following the processional, the seniors will remain standing, and men will remove their caps for the "Crimson Banner," salute to the flag, and class aspiration.
  • Seniors will sit in unison following the principal's lead.
  • After presentation of the class by Jeff Meisenheimer, Principal, seniors will stand by row and proceed across the stage and receive a diploma cover as their names are read from the cards. YOU MUST HAVE YOUR NAME CARD AND BE WITH YOUR MENTOR IN ORDER TO CROSS THE STAGE.
  • Hand the name card to the appropriate reader at the left or right side of the stage.
  • Proceed to the center of the stage to receive diploma cover*.
  • Shake the hand of the LSR-7 Board of Education representative.
  • Walk down the center stage steps, greet your mentor and return to your original seat.
  • Seniors will rise for the affirmation at the end of the ceremony, and ushers will manage the recessional from back to front and direct graduates to the traditional "hat-toss" location. *Once completed, direction will be provided on where to pick up diplomas before leaving.
