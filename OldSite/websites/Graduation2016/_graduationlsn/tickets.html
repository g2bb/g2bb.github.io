Tickets and Seating

Reserving and Picking Up Tickets

Parents of seniors will be sent a letter regarding tickets containing the following important information:

  • The number of tickets per graduating student will be announced
  • Ticket pickup date is to be announced
  • Parents are required to show photo ID and sign for the tickets when they pick them up. Students will NOT be allowed to pick up tickets.
  • Remember that everyone is required to have a ticket for entrance to graduation.

Ticket Collection Procedures and Seating at Community of Christ Auditorium

  • Graduation announcements ordered through Jostens are NOT tickets toÂ the ceremony.
  • A ticket is not a reserved seat. A ticket gains one person per ticket entrance to the building. Tickets do not assign seats in the auditorium.
  • No one may gain admittance to the auditorium without a ticket in hand. One ticket per seat. Toddlers and infants require their own ticket if they are not in the lap of an adult - age limit for lap-seating is younger than 3 years of age.
  • Once a guest’s ticket stub has been collected and he/she needs to exit the building; a ticket stub will be given to the guest in order to exit and return. The stub will be collected upon return. Again no person will gain access to the building without a ticket in hand.
  • There is no "saving seats" at commencement. Guests will be reminded that they cannot save seats and noncompliance to this requrest will be referred to security.
  • ONCE THE CEREMONY HAS BEGUN, LATE GUESTS WILL BE DIRECTED TO BALCONY SEATING ONLY

Handicapped Seating (wheelchair accessibility)

  • Handicapped Seating (wheelchair accessibility) is available.
    The west entrance to the Auditorium has wheelchair accessibility and seating. This is the main entrance as most of the parking is available on the West Side. A circle drop-off area is at the west entrance. The auditorium has other handicapped seating on the floor level East Side. Elevators and ramps are available inside the auditorium. Parking is restricted to areas designated on the map as "Auditorium Parking" only.

Community of Christ Auditorium Access

  • The auditorium has four entrances: north, south, east, and west. The west entrance is the main parking area (off Crysler St.) of the auditorium. Reserved seating for graduates is provided. Uniformed police officers as well as off duty LSPD officers and a hired event security company will assist in crowd control, emergencies and traffic safety.
