More About Me

Who am I?

My name is Joe Hays and I am a Junior at Lee's Summit North High School. I am a computer enthusiast and I frequently take full advantage of the creative side of my brain.

What have I accomplished?

I have taken Computer Hardware and Operating Systems I and II and attained my "PC Pro" certification through that. I have also achieved the rank of Eagle Scout and have recently earned a bronze palm in the Boy Scout Troop 251. In addition to that I have earned my black belt in Tae-Kwon-Do.

What can I do?

I am experienced in programs such as Adobe Dreamweaver, Adobe Photoshop, and Brackets. I also have experience with the hardware side of computers.

Can I speak any other languages?

Technically yes. I studied Spanish for 2 years, however it has been 2 years since I have practiced. While it is possible for me to speak Spanish it is not plausible.

What does the future hold for me?

I am unsure as to what my career will be right now, but I am certain that computer technology will be an integral part of my future.

Need any help?

I am available most weekday afternoons and weekends to help with your computer needs in any way possible. Whether you need a website built, some recommendations on what products to purchase in order to meet your needs, speeding up a bogged down PC, fixing your broken computer, or even tutoring help with all things electonics, I can help. If you ever decide that you would like my assistance you can Email me at g2bb@aol.com.
