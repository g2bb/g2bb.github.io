AWD Course Application
Information

In this year-long course, students incorporate advanced web design techniques in the areas of html, CSS, image creation and manipulation, audio, video, and animation. Instructional strategies include teacher demonstration, peer teaching, and independent research of current web design principles using online articles and tutorials. This class is responsible for the creation and maintenance of some pages on the school website; therefore, good attendance is expected. Final evaluation includes a website portfolio project. It is highly recommended that students purchase their own 1 gig or greater memory stick. Eligible students can take this class for Articulated credit or Dual College Credit (up to 6 hours credit possible through Metropolitan Community College). This is a .5 weighted class.

Assignments will be based on skills and software as they are mastered and will be demonstrated in the form of projects. The Advanced Web Design class is responsible for various content and pages on the LSN school website. Major assignments and projects include planning, creating, and maintaining these sites to include but not limited to: homecoming, advanced web team, courtwarming, prom, and clubs or activities. Other websites may be included based on the needs of the school. Additional projects will include multimedia projects in graphic art, audio, video, and animation. As this is a weighted class, a research component will be required each semester.
