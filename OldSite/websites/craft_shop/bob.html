Jonathan Nash

Hello my name is Jonathan Nash and I am a graphic designer. I am a 2013 graduate of Rhode Island School of Design in the top 10% of my class. As a child Bob Ross's show on PBS inspired me to persue art. I have been doing graphics since Multimedia my freshman year (2005) in high school. While I am good with most types of graphics I specialize in logos/banner, photo editing, and material design. I hope you stay a while and check out all the amazing forms of art on our website.
