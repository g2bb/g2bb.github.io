Directions from Lee's Summit

(As suggested by Community of Christ Church)

  • Take 291 north and exit I-70 west to Independence, Missouri.
  • Take the Noland Road exit.
  • Turn right (north) on Noland Road to Walnut (approximately 9 stoplights).
  • Turn left (west) onto Walnut and go 1 mile to River.
  • Turn left (south) onto River (the Auditorium, with a green dome, is on your right).
  • Turn right (west) into the first parking lot entrance.

For alternative directions, view Driving Maps (pdf).
