Project Graduation Event Information

Monday, May 16, 2016 -- 9:30 PM to 3:30 AM

Matt Ross Community Center in Overland Park, KS

  • Meet in the East Events parking lot at 9:00 p.m.
  • Buses leave promptly at 9:30 p.m.
  • Buses leave Matt Ross Community Center at 3:00 AM Wednesday, May 20, 2015 and return to Lees Summit North.

***All students must ride the bus there and home. There are no exceptions to this rule unless there is a school-related conflict. Administrative permission is needed in those cases.

What to Do at Project Graduation

  • Basketball tournaments
  • Swim
  • Games (Obstacle courses, bingo, golf, movies, spin the money wheel, and more)
  • Eat great food
  • Win prizes

How to Buy a Ticket

  • Tickets go on sale in the Commons from April 28 - May 8
  • Or they can be purchased from Mrs. Collins, room 2303.
  • Seniors pay $10.00
  • Seniors Only! No guests allowed.
  • When you buy your ticket, you are assigned a bus. Be sure to get tickets together with the friends you would like to ride the bus with. There will be no switching busses once tickets are purchased.

Rules & Guidelines

  • NO changing buses.
  • NO smoking on the bus or at the Matt Ross Community Center.  This is a smoke-free facility.
  • ONLY basketball shoes will be allowed on the Matt Ross Community Center gym floor.
  • FOLLOW all school rules and direction of sponsors.  An administrator will be there.
