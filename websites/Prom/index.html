Prom 2015 - May 2

Where

Saturday, May 2, 2015
8:00 PM - 11:00 PM
John Knox Pavilion

Theme

This year's Prom theme is "Timeless".

Colors

The colors associated with this theme are: white, silver, black, and a pinkish maroon

Tickets

Where can I buy them?

During Lunch Shifts or Before 1st Hour in the Lobby Outside the Media Center.

When can I buy them?

Monday, April 27 – Friday, May 1

More Important Information Regarding Tickets

  • You will need your ticket AND your LSN student I.D. for admittance to prom.
  • Juniors, seniors and LSN dates wanting to attend Prom, must have all LSN obligations fulfilled by 3:00 p.m. on Friday, May 1, 2015 to Mrs. Stolfus in the front office. This includes detentions served, fines paid and all other obligations settled.
  • Tickets can be purchased with personal checks (made payable to LSNHS) or the correct amount of cash.
  • Tickets will not be sold the night of prom!

Guests

  • Junior and senior students are not required to have a date to attend prom.
  • Only one guest per LSN student is allowed.
  • All guests must be a minimum age of attendance of the classification of a freshman in high school. All prom guests MUST NOT have reached the age of 21 - NO EXCEPTIONS!!!
  • Out-of-school guests must provide their school I.D. or a valid picture I.D. (i.e. driver’s license) at the time of admittance.
  • Guest Forms
    • Out-of-school guests are permitted if the Out of School Guest Request form is returned to Mrs. Stolfus in the main office no later than 3:00pm on Wednesday, April 29, 2015.
    • It is required that students with dates, who are NOT LSNHS students, complete a date request form.
    • Please fill in the form completely! Form must be approved. Please remember that it is your responsibility to obtain the signature of the other School’s Administrator. Please do not ask Ms. Stolfus to fax the form for you.

Out of School Guest Form:
http://lsnhs.leesummit.k12.mo.us/lsn/prom2012/Prom%20Out%20of%20School%20Dance%20Guest%20Request.pdf

Dress and Attire

Formal wear is required, although gentlemen may wear suits instead of tuxedos.

Parents

Parents are welcome to stop by and visit prom. We welcome the opportunity for you to see our decorations and students dressed so beautifully. Out of respect to the event, we ask our visitors and guests at prom to wear the proper attire—business professional. Please, no jeans, shorts or athletic attire should be worn. This is a formal occasion for our students. If parents or guardians have any questions regarding this event, please contact one of the school administrators or Joy Reed, Prom sponsor at joy.reed@lsr7.net
